# 💧 Drop By Drop — School Water Conservation Campaign

**Drop By Drop** is a student-led awareness website created as part of the **Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives Team Project (0457)**.  
The project focuses on **water conservation**, combining local and global perspectives with real-world action.

🌍 **Live website:** https://adrianmehran.github.io/DropByDrop  
📚 **Course:** Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives  
📌 **Component:** Team Project – Evidence of Action

---

## 🌱 Project Aim

The aim of this project is to **raise awareness about water scarcity and water waste** among students and families, and to encourage **simple, practical actions** that reduce water consumption.

The website supports a wider school campaign that included:
- Awareness posters
- A student survey
- A school stand at a Winter Fête
- Fundraising for the charity **#TeamWater** through the stand at our school's Winter Fête

## 🧠 Local & Global Perspectives

- **Local perspective:**  
  Water usage and awareness in schools and households in Nice, France

- **Global perspective:**  
  Global water scarcity, unequal access to clean water, and the impact of climate change on water resources

## 📊 Impact & Outcomes

- **96** survey responses collected  
- **650+** people reached through the campaign  
- **351€** raised and donated to **#TeamWater**, helping provide clean water access to people in need  

All data displayed on the website reflects real results from the project.
