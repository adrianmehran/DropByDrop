Why it matters

Water scarcity is made worse by drought, climate change, and rising demand. In Nice, tourism and hot summers can increase pressure on local water use. Our project focuses on what habits students and families can realistically change to save water #DropByDrop.

Easy ways to save water

Small actions. Big impact when everyone does them.

Turn off the tap
While brushing teeth or soaping hands. It can save 12L of water/day!
Report leaks
A dripping tap can waste a surprising amount over time (15L/day).
Shorter showers
Try reducing by 1‚Äì2 minutes this week. This would save 20L/shower!
Refill bottles
Refill instead of buying new ones. Saves 3L by avoiding manufacturing.
Full loads
Dishwasher (save 10L) / washing machine (save 25L): fewer cycles = less water.
Be mindful
Close taps fully. Don‚Äôt ‚Äúleave it running‚Äù. Running taps waste 6L/hour.
